What is the Booster Club?

The JBHS Orchestra Booster Club consists of parent volunteers who support the orchestra program. The booster clubâ€™s primary goal is to raise money on behalf of the orchestra. With this money, the boosters award Senior scholarships, put on social events, purchase items for the program such as musical instruments, uniforms, strings, and instrument repairs, and help with travel and food expenses. Parents are encouraged to join the booster club and attend meetings in order to contribute ideas, lend their expertise, and share in the work of planning and executing various activities for our orchestra students.
